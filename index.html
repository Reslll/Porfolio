About me

As a frontend developer who believes that learning never ends, I strive to learn something new every day in order to move forward. Through various team and personal projects as an undergraduate student, I have come to appreciate the power and importance of communication. As part of a team project, I primarily used JavaScript, MySQL, and PHP to build a ride-share system. We made efforts to implement more realistic features such as using the Google Maps API to calculate fares based on the distance between the starting and ending points. Additionally, I have created several web pages using HTML/CSS and PHP to communicate with users. Rather than simply making the product look good, I invested significant time and attention to UX in order to provide users with a good experience.
